Here are a few things I enjoy doing

Music

Music is one of my dearest passions. I love listening to music, playing music, and discovering new music. I've been a drummer for over ten years now and let me tell you there's nothing greater than letting off steam behind the drums or grooving along to a fun new song. The fellow in that picture is Buddy Rich, he was a prolific jazz drummer back in the 70's. His speed and style is still unmatched to this day. Whenever I want to inspire myself to improve my drumming I'll watch old videos of his drumming.

As far as listening to music goes, my style is very eclectic. I'll listen to just about anything; heavy metal, jazz, rap, funk, japanese fusion jazz, etc. Yes I'll even listen to (some) country.

If you want to be my best friend, send me your favorite song or band!

Swimming

Swimming is a fairly new passion of mine, for the last 7 years I've been a couch for a youth team. Now I grew up in rural farm towns that didn't have access to a swim team. Now I took lessons when I was little so I've always loved the water, but I didn't know jack about competitive swimming or have any knowledge of technique. Fastforward to high school where I got my first summer job at the local gym. I was mainly a cleaner but I also helped the swim teachers with their classes.

When I started coaching I had to study up on proper techniques and coaching practices, I've come a long way since then. The kids have started calling me the Fun Coach.

Thanks to coaching I've develped a better sense of discipline and enjoyment. I push my swimmers but more importantly I make sure they're enjoying it and having fun. If we don't get any enjoyment out of what we do then we lose the drive to improve.

Games

Oh yeah I love video games! I'm taking a computer science class, why wouldn't I also enjoy one of the main appeals of having a computer?

Gaming has helped me improve my problem-solving skills, group coordination, and helps me identify patterns. I've met a few of my dearest friends through these gaming communities, not that I have to be friends with whoever I work with but it's a nice bonus. Working together to overcome challenges makes the reward even greater.
